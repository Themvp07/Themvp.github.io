Self-sovereign digital identity is vital in the digital age. It allows people to control their personal data, improve privacy and reduce online fraud. Provides portability, autonomy, and security by allowing users to manage their information across multiple platforms in an interoperable manner.

KayTrust is a trusted platform that allows you to manage the digital identities of your customers in a safe, reliable and end-to-end manner. It allows customers to do a completely digital onboarding without leaving their homes, thus maintaining the flow of sales in any circumstance.

KayTrust is powered by blockchain and allows multiple types of authentication, working with biometric engines and high-quality standards. It has a centralized and decentralized Single sign-On process. And of course, everything is stored in the cloud and attested in the blockchain.
